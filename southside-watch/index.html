What's Really Happening in Southside Virginia

Data centers bring promises — but residents need to ask hard questions about who actually benefits, who absorbs the costs, who makes the decisions, and what the real-world impacts will be.

💰

Who Really Benefits?

Developers often hide behind LLCs. End users — major tech giants — are rarely disclosed. Who is collecting the economic gains from Southside Virginia's land and infrastructure?

About This Project

Who We Are and Why This Matters

📋

Public Records First

Every claim is sourced. We cite public records, FOIA responses, meeting minutes, permit filings, and verified community reports. We distinguish confirmed facts from rumors.

⚖️

Not Anti-Technology

This project is about accountability, not opposition. Data centers can bring real benefits — but those benefits must be weighed honestly against real costs, with residents at the table.

🏘️

Resident-First

Southside Virginia communities deserve the same transparency and protections as any other region. We advocate for full disclosure, community input, and fair deals — before approval, not after.
